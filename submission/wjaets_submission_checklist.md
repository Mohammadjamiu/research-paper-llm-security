# WJAETS Submission Checklist

## Files prepared

- `submission/wjaets_manuscript.md`: manuscript content arranged in WJAETS structure.
- `submission/cover_letter_wjaets.md`: cover letter draft.
- `submission/author_declaration_wjaets.md`: author declaration draft.

## Still needed before submission

- Confirm all co-authors have reviewed and approved the final manuscript.
- Convert the manuscript to `.docx` format.
- Apply Times New Roman 12 pt, double spacing, justified alignment, A4 paper, and 1 inch margins.
- Add automatic page numbers at the bottom right.
- Add automatic line numbering with restart on each page.
- Ensure figures are at least 300 dpi if converted from SVG to PNG.
- Add DOI information to references where available.
- Use the journal's official cover letter and author declaration form if their downloaded templates differ from these drafts.

## Recommended submission category

Full length research article.
