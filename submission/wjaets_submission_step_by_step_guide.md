## 2. Prepare the Manuscript in Word Format

WJAETS accepts manuscripts in `.doc` or `.docx` format only. Do not submit a PDF as the main manuscript.

Use the following formatting:

- A4 page size.
- 1 inch margins on all sides.
- Times New Roman, 12 pt.
- Double line spacing.
- Justified text alignment.
- Automatic page numbers at the bottom right.
- Automatic line numbering, restarting on each page.
- Tables and figures inserted close to the text where they are discussed.

Use `submission/wjaets_manuscript.md` as the source content when preparing the `.docx` file.

## 4. Prepare the Cover Letter

Use `submission/cover_letter_wjaets.md` as the completed cover letter draft.

If the journal provides an official cover letter template, paste the completed content into their template.

## 5. Prepare the Author Declaration Form

Use `submission/author_declaration_wjaets.md` as the completed declaration draft.

If the journal provides an official declaration form, copy the details from this file into the official form.

The corresponding author should sign the declaration before submission.

## 6. Final Pre-Submission Checks

Before submitting, check the following:

- The manuscript title is correct.
- All author names and affiliations are correct.
- All author emails are correct.
- The abstract is below 250 words.
- The manuscript has 4-6 keywords.
- The manuscript is not under review elsewhere.
- All figures and tables are cited in the text.
- References are numbered in order of appearance.
- The conflict of interest statement is included.
- The acknowledgement section is included.
- The manuscript file is in `.doc` or `.docx` format.

## 7. Where to Submit

Submit the manuscript through one of the WJAETS submission channels listed in the journal instructions:

- Email submission: `editor@wjaets.com`
- Online submission link: use the **Submit articles** or **Submit Manuscript** link on the WJAETS website.

If submitting by email, attach:

- Final manuscript in `.doc` or `.docx` format.
- Cover letter.
- Author declaration form.
- Any required journal forms.
- Separate figure files if requested by the editor.

## 10. After Submission

After submission, WJAETS should send a manuscript number to the corresponding author. Save this manuscript number for all future communication.

Do not submit the same manuscript to another journal while waiting for WJAETS's decision.

If accepted, review the page proof carefully because only minor corrections are usually allowed at the proof stage.
